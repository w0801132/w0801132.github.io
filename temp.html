Having been born and raised in Southwest Florida, I feel I had firsthand experience of the trend noticeable in the first graph: namely, that temperatures tend to be higher towards the equator and drop off as you approach the poles. Because daily weather can be impacted by many variables, there will always be outliers that are higher or lower than the general trend, but the trend is still clear.
