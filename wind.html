Vs. Wind



Wind speed surprised me a little, only because the equator is famous for the doldrums. But I did a bit of reading and it turns out that the trade winds on either side of the equator can be quite strong, so that then makes sense when we’re looking at a random selection of cities at various latitudes. It would be interesting if we’d happened upon a city exactly on the equator (the internet tells me there’s only one: Pontianak, Indonesia) to see what their wind speeds are like today. (Also, when I looked up the doldrums I found the ‘horse latitudes’ which are areas about 30 degrees north or south of the equator where sailors can also be becalmed.)
